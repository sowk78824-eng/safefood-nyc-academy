## Common Questions

### Q: Do I need to share my location?
**A:** No! Click "Open Google Maps" without location. Google Maps will ask you to select your starting point.

### Q: Does it work on my phone?
**A:** Yes! Works on all iOS, Android, and desktop devices.

### Q: What if I deny location permission?
**A:** The app shows demo data and lets you use Google Maps anyway.

### Q: Is my location stored?
**A:** No! It's only used to generate the Maps URL, then deleted from memory.

### Q: Does it work offline?
**A:** Generating the directions URL works offline, but opening Maps requires internet.

### Q: Why two buttons (Google/Apple Maps)?
**A:** Apple Maps works better on iOS. Google Maps works everywhere.
